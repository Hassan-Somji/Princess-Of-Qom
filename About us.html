Who Are We?

A group of diverse Howza students, studying at the Islamic Seminary of Qom with different backgrounds striving to acquire and spread the teachings of the Pure Muhammadan Islam.

We aim to feature authentic, reliable, and relevant sources of Islamic knowledge and bring it to the masses familiar with English language. We plan to export the knowledge of Islamic sciences ranging from the Islamic belief system to the Islamic political system.

As Howza students residing in the Holy city of Qom, we have access to many knowledgeable personalities and resources. With your prayers and tawfeeq from Allah (swt), we are committed to bring this knowledge to you. We pray that Allah gives us the strength to carry out this important task and that this becomes the source of pleasure for Imam Mahdi (atfs). May Allah hasten his return.

Our Focus

With the help of Allah, we plan to focus on the following areas of Islamic sciences on Princess of qom. Insha’Allah.

Islamic Theology

Providing answers to the foundational questions of one’s life and belief system.

Islamic Philosophy

Setting the worldview of believers by applying logic through critical thinking.

Islamic Spirituality

Explaining the ingredients of pure Islamic spirituality in which Almighty Allah is the goal.

Islamic Political Thought

Analyzing and dissecting the framework of the political system and process in Islam.
